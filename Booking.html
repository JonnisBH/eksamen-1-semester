INFORMATION

Metro Café is the place to go if you want something hot to drink or eat. If you want a place to get some work done, this is the place to go! With soft music and good service, we are the best place for those who want a break from the everyday stress. You need an escape from the real world? Metro Café is the place to go!

CAPACITY

The least amount of people you can fit per table is 1 person, the most is 6. We have single tables in the corners and by the windows, where you can relax and do your own thing or be productive with work etc.
